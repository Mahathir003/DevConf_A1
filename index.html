Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before attending DevConf.

Who can attend DevConf?

Anyone interested in software development, AI, cloud computing, cybersecurity, or technology is welcome to attend.

Will the conference sessions be recorded?

Yes. All attendees will receive access to the recorded sessions after the conference.

Is food included with my ticket?

Yes. Lunch, coffee breaks, and refreshments are included for all registered attendees.

Where is the conference being held?

The conference will take place at the International Convention Center with easy access to hotels and public transportation.

Are refunds available for purchased tickets?

Yes. Refund requests are accepted up to 14 days before the event. After that, tickets are non-refundable but can be transferred to another attendee.
